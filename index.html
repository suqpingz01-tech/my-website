Day 21 Game Name : My Dystopian Robot Girlfriend PC AND ANDROID

A dystopian visual novel set in an alternate 2023 where sexbots have been banned worldwide.
After finding a rare abandoned sexbot, an unemployed young man is pulled into a dangerous world of black markets,
rival factions, and difficult choices that will shape his future.

Day 20 Game Name : Miss neko pirate PC ONLY

Take control of a betrayed pirate captain in this 2D top-down action game.
Explore a dangerous island filled with enemies, engage in intense hack-and-slash battles, dodge
incoming attacks, and gather a crew of colorful beast-girl allies as you fight to recover your stolen
ship.

Day 19 Game Name : Zarie: The Story of Sin PC ONLY

Explore a mysterious monochrome world and uncover the truth behind your fate.
Guided by Zarie, a self-proclaimed deity of death, you travel through Hell in search of judgment,
facing the secrets of your past along the way.

Day 18 Game Name : Isekai quest PC ONLY

After a sudden trip to a fantasy world, an unlikely protagonist chooses a far less

Day 17 Game Name : MiSide PC ONLY

before taking a dark and unsettling turn. Trapped inside a digital world, players must
uncover its secrets while dealing with a mysterious anime girl.
