Wet Nightmares combines psychological horror with mature themes, placing players in a series of unsettling and disturbing situations. The experience features voiced scenes, atmospheric sound design, and narrative descriptions from the protagonist's perspective. The game explores themes of manipulation, power imbalance, loss of control, and dark fantasy scenarios. Throughout the story, players will encounter both horror-driven and adult-oriented content designed to create tension and discomfort. Its horror elements include grotesque transformations, nightmare imagery, psychological distress, and other disturbing situations that contribute to the game's unsettling atmosphere.
